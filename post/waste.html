I've been to many psychologists, but it always feels like I'm not taken seriously by any of them about anything I say, regardless of the nature or language. actually that's not entirely true. they often like to focus on the tiniest and unimportant things. they like to focus on my everyday things, when I can manage coping with them relatively well.

it must be my fault.
