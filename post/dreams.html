dreams

📅 July 2, 2026

such a precious precious song to start off this melancholic post with.

I dreamt again. oh my gosh, it felt so nice to dream again... I felt like when I was 15 again. as if I was really almost 15 again.

the dream didn't make much sense. there are things I'd rather not share about it, but I remember a part where I was exploring abandoned lab facilities with friends and we came across geese. we ended up playing football with them. and... what was really weird is that it became an epic, cinematic music video while I was still experiencing it from my own perspective, with occassional shifts to cinematic perspectives. it was very much like something Riot Games would make and the track was so really good. I felt like I was at an all-time high. there was this disk mechanic where the geese would block with some sort of 2D spinning disk (like a vinyl record) and I would press down on it with my fingers and that would damage it, causing the scratched part to go through the entire disk and therefore damage the goose standing there. it was really interesting, a game-within-a-game. but the track... gosh. I realized while trying to force them to stop using the disk that I was dreaming and I realized the track was a fabrication of my mind. it felt like my mind telling me my creative block doesn't mean I'm not capable of it. it felt like an indirect communication with the depths.

I just remembered, I also had dreams of people from my past... but it was more sad than anything. And I keep... keep dreaming of being in some sort of educational institution and getting into altercations with people I hate. often I punch them bloody. sometimes though, I just scream, I just yell, curse. it doesn't matter, nobody really pays attention. unless I'm beating them bloody, in which case... yeah. but even as we go at each-other, it always feels like my punches are weak. like I'm underwater trying my hardest to punch while the other person punches like bullets.

such silly dreams, right? idek what to make of them! I did like the goose one. it went on for longer. there were so many things. we explored a lot. it felt like an open-world right there at arm's reach... grass I stood on, upright and proud, excited to see what the world had to offer.

you know what I miss? tears... I miss crying. I miss the feeling of crying. I am getting tired of the sexually tense settings. I don't want to dream of that anymore. not now. it does help me fall asleep, but not the dreams. I want to see people I miss. I want to see people who've taught me despair. but I'm not complaining... my last dream really inspired me. I'm not sure for what, but it inspired me. could never stop creating. will never stop creating, even if nobody'll know or see. I'll die creating.

I'm not sure what else I can tell you about this, but know, I felt.

— end of line —
