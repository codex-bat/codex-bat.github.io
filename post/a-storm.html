home about me

a storm

📅 June 28, 2026

it's 2:43... I came home after 1AM, because my quixotic idealist wouldn't stop calling, playwright either... they were pretty quick to notice i was gone. I should've known that they'd suspect it in such a turn of events. I came home, not planning on staying, just as a refuge for a few hours until I leave. I'm not planning on sleeping, I want to be wide awake. however, just now it started raining heavily... like pouring buckets with thunder and everything. it was right when I was feeling... lost. and now, after opening the window to it and admiring it a bit, it has, just as abruptly as it came, stopped.

the idea of moving over to quixotic idealist really tickles my fancy, right now... it would lend me the freedom to work on my projects, guilt-free and without the pressure of the 2.0 release of playwright constantly looming over me. as much as I love her, it's hard for me to be with her all day. even though I know playwright is basically left alone with her 24/7, as her associate is pretty absent most of the time. speaking of which: the paper only gives her until the 30th to show up --- just two days after playwright 2.0 release's birthday... what a memorable vth birthday party this must be.

walking around on the streets with the semi-committed plan of staying, was quite eliberating. it felt unsafe and unsure... but I also felt free to explore anything I desire. now, my eyelids feel pretty heavy and I have a raging lust in me. I don't know where it came from, but I'm pretty sure it's a response to the situation. ig my body is trying to find a pressure valve that can release the piled up emotional steam from the crisis. I think it might've been inappropriate of me to say that, but I don't really mind. I'm a free, open book for you. I want you to know me.

I hadn't thought about one thing: tomorrow is playright 2.0's birthday... it'd be a shame to leave her on her own birthday, but I really... really need an escape. I'm not joking, I'm so exhausted and worried from this place. when the associate came home, he saw that the door was closed to his "workshop" room that he's been using as his bedroom and that the blinds were also lowered; that was the doing of 2.0, who decided it'd be a good idea to mix wax from the different candles and pour water on it. however, he didn't trust that story and decided to look through the room. it was silly and I found it understandable, but also kind of scary... the distrust in the family has been there for a while and I've always tried bridging that gap by pushing playwright to talk to the associate honestly and with openness and rational... however as she stated it (and she's most probably right, seeing as how unpredictable he's become): "nothing works on him. emotions don't work on him, because he can't empathize and logic doesn't work on him either, because it's not 'his way'.". it honestly all fits with him. several times, the phrase "it's his way or the highway" has been mentioned and he flaunted his power over us about the fact that "he could throw us out at any moment, because it's his house" and that I'm "only staying here, because you're playwright's [TITLE]". I was used to it, but it was mostly just big talk. I knew him as someone who had an insecure, fragile ego and would feel threatened by my presence in some way. perhaps my resilience and ability to independently decide for myself was scary. whatever it may be, this abstract behaviour from the past is now an actual legal case that might ground playwright into forcefully staying in this place. I cannot change my own plans after the 25th of July, however... despite my empathy, I'd ruin my own life sticking around. I explicitly stated this already, as I've been already asked to stay.

I worry that I'm overthinking it... but I really think perhaps staying at a café or a library and doing stuff there will help ease my mind a little. and I'm still trying to figure out if going over to quixotic idealist could prove to be a good idea... it would be the very apartment that'd remind me of these events... OH SHIT, and what about the report?! oh my gosh, if I went, I'd be unable to file my report in time. it'd be a hassle, because I wouldn't be near the capital... but in an entirely different regime.

I have to think about this some more. thankfully, I have a few hours until my potential train at 5:25AM. perhaps I could just train surf. going through random lines just to kill some time. so much logistics... I just need a break. please just allow me to breathe a bit...
